Who We Are

Crafted with passion,
served with pride.

Big Short Pizza is a fast-casual pizzeria serving delicious, made-to-order pies with freshly prepared in-house dough daily. Perfect for a quick lunch, snack, or dinner on-the-go at your corporate campus.

We're an authorized direct party of Amul, bringing premium ice creams, chocolates, cookies, and dairy treats to corporate campuses across Pune. We supply to Sodexo India Pvt Ltd and distribute for Parle, Britannia, Sunfeast & Unibic.

Vision & Mission

"From a neighbourhood kirana to a Fortune 500 canteen — we serve them all."
