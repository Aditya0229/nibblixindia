Who We Are

A cafeteria & MRP counter
in every company.

Nibblix India Pvt Ltd is a Pune-based food services company focused on one mission: setting up and running cafeteria counters and MRP product tuckshops in as many corporate campuses as possible.

We own Big Short Pizza — our in-house brand known for fresh dough prepared daily, never frozen. Alongside our own brand, we distribute ice cream and a wide range of FMCG products through partnerships with Amul, ITC, Cadbury, Yoga Bar, and more.

Whether it's a pizza counter, an ice cream parlour, or a fully stocked tuckshop — we handle everything from procurement and staffing to daily operations, ensuring cleanliness, quality, and hospitality at every touchpoint.

Supply Chain
