Who We Are

A cafeteria & MRP counter
in every company.

Nibblix India Pvt Ltd is a Pune-based food services company focused on one mission: setting up and running cafeteria counters and MRP product tuckshops in as many corporate campuses as possible.

We own Big Short Pizza — our in-house brand known for fresh dough prepared daily, never frozen. Alongside our own brand, we distribute ice cream and a wide range of FMCG products through partnerships with Amul, ITC, Cadbury, Yoga Bar, and more.

Whether it's a pizza counter, an ice cream parlour, or a fully stocked tuckshop — we handle everything from procurement and staffing to daily operations, ensuring cleanliness, quality, and hospitality at every touchpoint.

Mission & Values

Why we exist.

"A cafeteria and MRP tuckshop in every company we can reach."

Our mission is simple — set up and operate high-quality food counters and product tuckshops in as many corporate campuses as possible. From a 200-person office to a 10,000-employee tech park, every workspace deserves great food, clean service, and a well-stocked counter. Our vision is to take this brand global.

🧼

Cleanliness

FSSAI licensed. Daily sanitation, food-grade equipment, gloves & hairnets mandatory at every counter.

🙏

Hospitality

Trained staff who greet with a smile. Corporate employees deserve restaurant-level warmth.

⭐

Quality

Same fresh taste every day. Strict sourcing, daily checks, and standardized recipes across branches.

📈

Growth

Our vision is to go global — starting with every corporate campus in Pune, then India, then beyond.

Shipping & Logistics
